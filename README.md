## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Start adding tasks and earning XP!
3. Your progress is automatically saved locally in your browser

## XP System

- **Easy tasks**: +10 XP
- **Medium tasks**: +25 XP
- **Hard tasks**: +50 XP

Level up every 100 XP!

## Badges

Earn badges by:
- Completing your first task
- Reaching Level 5, 10, and beyond
- Maintaining streaks (7 days, 30 days)
- Completing multiple tasks
- Completing hard challenges

## Privacy

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No data is sent to any server. Clear your browser data to reset your progress.
