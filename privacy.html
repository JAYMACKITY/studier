Privacy Policy

1. No Personal Data

Studier does not require login or any personal information. All task data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and cannot be accessed by anyone else.

2. Data Control

Users have full control of their data. Clearing browser storage or cache will erase tasks and XP.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party services or analytics. No tracking is performed.

4. Security

While we aim to provide a safe app environment, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Users are responsible for protecting their devices.

5. Updates

Privacy practices may change with updates. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
